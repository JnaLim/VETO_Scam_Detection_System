What To Do If You've Been Scammed

Follow this structured guide to secure your accounts, report the incident, and prevent further loss. Stay calm and act quickly.

Secure Accounts

  • Contact your bank immediately
  • Change passwords for all critical accounts (email, bank, social)
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Report Incident

  • File police report at PDRM CCID: 03-2610-1559
  • Report to Bank Negara: 1-300-88-5465
  • Contact Cyber999: 1-300-88-2999

Gather Evidence

  • Save chat logs and transaction receipts
  • Document all interactions with the suspected scammer
  • Note payment and transfer details clearly

The Mechanics Of Scams

♡

Romance Scam

Scammers build fake emotional relationships to gain trust and ask for money.

▥

Bank Scam

Fraudsters impersonate bank staff through calls, text messages, or fake alerts.

✉

Phishing

Fake emails, texts, or websites are used to steal login credentials and personal data.

🛒

Shopping Scam

Fake online shops offer attractive deals but deliver nothing or poor-quality items.

♜

Customer Service Scam

Fraudsters pose as support teams or fake sellers to trick users into making payments.

▣

Delivery Scams

Fake shipping notifications ask victims to pay fees or install malicious software.

🎟

Lottery

Victims are told they won a prize but must pay fees or provide details first.

☼

Charity

Scammers exploit sympathy by pretending to raise funds for emergencies or relief efforts.

Stay vigilant. Take control. Verified authorities are here to support you.

Security Guides & Anti-Fraud Center

○

WhatsApp Security Guides

  • Enable two-step verification.
  • Scan images for suspicious content.
  • Ignore group invites from unknown contacts.
▤

SMS Fraud Prevention

  • Detect phishing links.
  • Verify service notifications.
  • Do not share verification codes.
♙

Social Media Account Safety

  • Watch for impersonation scams.
  • Check login activity.
  • Enable enhanced privacy settings.
✉

Email Phishing Defense

  • Inspect sender address.
  • Scan all attachments.
  • Recognize common red flags.
▥

Online Banking Login Safety

  • Use only official apps or websites.
  • Avoid login links from messages.
  • Check account activity notifications.
▭

Card & Transaction Protection

  • Review transactions regularly.
  • Enable spending alerts.
  • Never share PIN or CVV.
▣

Loan & Investment Scam Awareness

  • Question guaranteed returns.
  • Verify financial institutions.
  • Avoid upfront processing fees.
☏

Bank Call & OTP Scam Defense

  • Do not share OTP over calls.
  • Verify the caller independently.
  • Report suspicious banking calls.
